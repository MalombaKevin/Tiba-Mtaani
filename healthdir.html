Tibapedia

Tibapedia is an initiative by Tiba Mtaani to build an online directory to enable citizens to access
most if not all information concerning health.It is both an information and education platform that helps stakeholders
access information such as the hospitals in a specific area, to the various fields of medicine and diseases
